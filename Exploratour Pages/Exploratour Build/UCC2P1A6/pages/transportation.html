Transportation

How you move through a place shapes how you experience it. Fast isn’t always better — sometimes slower lets you see more.

Ways to Get Around

🚆 Public Transport

Trains, buses, and metros — usually the fastest and most local way to move.

🚶 Walking

The best way to actually feel a place instead of just passing through it.

🚕 Rideshare / Taxis

Convenient when you're tired or need to get somewhere quickly.

🚗 Car Rentals

Best for road trips or places where public transport is limited.

Travel Smarter

🗺️ Learn the System

A few minutes understanding transit maps saves a lot of time later.

⏰ Avoid Peak Hours

Rush hour can completely change your experience.

📍 Stay Central

Where you stay affects how much you’ll need to travel.
