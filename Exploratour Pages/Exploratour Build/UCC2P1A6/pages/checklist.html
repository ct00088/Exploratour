Travel Checklist

The small things are what people forget. Having everything ready makes your trip smoother and way less stressful.

Before You Leave

  • Passport or ID
  • Flight confirmation
  • Hotel reservation
  • Travel insurance

Packing Essentials

  • Clothes for the weather
  • Toiletries
  • Chargers
  • Comfortable shoes

Extras

  • Snacks
  • Headphones
  • Portable charger
  • Backup cash/card
